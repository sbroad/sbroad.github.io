Profile

Incorporated in the year 1981, at Jalandhar, (Punjab, India), we, “Sbrod Metal Works,” are engaged in manufacturing & supplying the finest quality range of Brass Metal Product, CP Product, Brass Product, Rack Bolt, Jet Spray Set, CP and Brass Taper Cock and Connection Pipe. The offered products are designed and fabricated at our high-tech manufacturing unit in compliance with international quality standards. In order to manufacture these products, we use supreme class raw material and latest technology. Further, the raw material we use in production process is sourced from reliable vendors of the industry under the supervision of our highly skilled procuring agents. We offer these products various specifications to fulfill the varied needs of the clients. Our products are highly acknowledged by our clients for their features like high tensile strength, robust construction, fine finish, dimensional accuracy, corrosion resistant finish and long lasting shine.

Located at Jalandhar, (Punjab, India), we have set up a sophisticated infrastructure that is segregated our infrastructure into different functional units which enables us in catering to the ever-changing demands of clients in an efficient manner. Our infrastructure is divided into manufacturing, quality testing, stock room & packaging, etc. With the support of these units, we are able to bring forth products that are unmatched in terms of quality and reliability. All these units are equipped with different kinds of requisite machines and technology. We are supported by a team of skilled professionals, who possess immense expertise in their respective domains. Our professionals help us in stay ahead of our competitors in the industry. They work is close sync with the clients to understand their precise requirements and offer them suitable products.

We have deployed quality testing unit that is governed by our experienced team of quality controllers. Our quality controllers strictly watch each stage of the production process and check finished products on various quality parameters. In order to deliver these products in timely manner, we have a wide distribution network which is manned by our experienced delivery agents. Our foremost objective is to provide utmost level of satisfaction to the clients. This objective is achieved by taking clients’ feedback and we make any possible change in our products range. Due to our ethical business policies, competitive price structure, transparent dealings and client-centric approach, we are constantly upgrading our list of clients spread across the world.

Under the guidance of our mentor, “Mr. Kewal Krishan Verma (Partner)”, we are successfully progressing in the industry. His broad industrial experienced and knowledge enables us to meet clients’ specific requirements in an efficient and economical manner.

Our Quality Assurance

The quality process starts from the very first process of manufacturing; we are dedicated to using only high quality selective raw materials that are being source from the reliable vendors. Standard Inspection according to quality standards and testing of finished products in our sophisticated instrumentation lab ensure standard quality, which we guarantee. Each finished bundle is packed separately, which makes it export worthy.
